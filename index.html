PhD Student · Computer Science

Sapienza University of Rome

I am a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, supervised by Prof. Iacopo Masi at the OmnAI Lab.

My research is driven by the broad goal of developing machine learning systems that are secure, safe, robust, and trustworthy. Within this broader domain, adversarial robustness, machine unlearning, test-time adaptation, model hallucinations, and defenses against unauthorized AI manipulation.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Kashmir and a Master's degree from Sapienza University of Rome. Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer in the fintech industry, contributing to data-driven solutions, and as a technical content writer communicating complex topics in data science and machine learning to a broader audience.
